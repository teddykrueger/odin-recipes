Master Sokka's Cactus Juice

“Drink cactus juice! It’ll quench ya. Nothing’s quenchier. It’s the quenchiest!” -Sokka

History of the Drink

Cactus Juice is a naturally occurring liquid found in certain succulent plants of the Si Wong Desert. It contains hallucinogens which cause those who consume it to temporarily fall into a deranged mental state.

In Season 2 Episode 11 of Avatar: The Last Airbender, Sokka and Momo consume juice from a cactus to quench their thirst. Little did they know the plant possesses hallucinogenic effects when consumed which led to an afternoon of tripping in the desert for the pair.

Keep reading for the steps to replicate the juice and enjoy the quenchiest quench you've ever quenched!

Ingredients

  • 5 limes (with skin and juice)
  • 5 lemons (with skin and juice)
  • 200g caster sugar
  • small piece of ginger; grated
  • 4 cups of distilled water
  • (optional) mint leaves

Steps

  1. Add the lemons, limes, grated ginger, sugar, and a splash of water to a pan on medium heat; whisk and bring to a boil.
  2. Make sure to stir well as the sugar and fruit can burn easily.
  3. Let the mixture thicken, adding more water if it gets to thick.
  4. Simmer for about 10 minutes on a low heat, then remove from heat once the mixture is slightly runny and syrupy in texture.
  5. Let drink cool for 5 minutes then sift and mix with remaining water.
  6. Add a few mint leaves to the mixture then pour into a glass and serve.
